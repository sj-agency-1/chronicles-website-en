2. COMPREHENSIVE INDUSTRY AUDIT

It's not that the system is blind, but that you lack an independent, external view. My audit services are not about checking boxes, but about scanning the business for hidden risks and unrealized opportunities.

Basic Audit Formats:

Remote Audit: Fee starting from $8,000. Completion time—up to 14 business days. Includes a final report and an optional online consultation on its results. Regulated by a Public Offer Agreement.

On-Site Audit: Fee starting from $25,000 + Travel & Logistics Requirements. Duration—up to 3 days, including travel time. Regulated by a full Service Agreement.

Specialized Audit Areas (Goals):

Manufacturing Audit: Analysis of production processes, equipment, cost of goods sold (COGS), and technology. Goal: Cost optimization and increased productivity.

Operations Audit: Diagnostics of business processes, internal regulations, and document flow. Goal: Increased operational efficiency and reduced transaction costs.

Marketing Audit: In-depth research of the market, niche, positioning, and marketing strategy. Goal: Identification of weaknesses, determination of growth areas, and improved Marketing ROI.

Advertising and Digital Audit: Analysis of advertising campaigns, funnels, KPIs, and creative materials. Goal: Identification of ineffective channels, clarification of the Target Audience (TA) profile, and increased conversion rates.

Commercial Audit: Evaluation of the CRM system, sales funnel, scripts, and sales department metrics. Goal: Growth in profitability, revenue, and optimization of commercial processes.

Logistics Audit: Audit of the full cycle: procurement, warehouse logistics, transportation, and supply chain management. Goal: Reduction of logistics costs and increased inventory turnover.

HR Audit: Analysis of personnel management systems, motivation, KPIs, and personnel documentation flow. Goal: Increased employee productivity and reduced staff turnover.

IT Audit: Evaluation of IT infrastructure, systems, cybersecurity, licenses, and User Experience (UI/UX). Goal: Ensuring the stability, security, and efficiency of the IT environment.

Financial Audit: Verification of financial statements, accounting, and cash flow. Goal: Confirmation of report reliability, identification of financial leaks, and optimization of cash flows.

Legal Audit: Analysis of contracts, constitutional documents, corporate structure, and litigation risks. Goal: Assessment of the legal integrity of the business and minimization of legal risks.

Investment Audit (Due Diligence Lite): Comprehensive assessment of a company’s finances, legal risks, and business potential. Goal: Providing the investor or buyer with an independent expert assessment of assets.
