# ELYQO – An Angel Investor Community

The ELYQO platform is my original project, serving as an exclusive community where private investors (angel investors) connect with innovators seeking funding.

Every project or startup featured on the platform undergoes comprehensive and rigorous due diligence by a professional team, including legal experts, financiers, analysts, auditors, marketers, and advertisers.

The platform ensures legal integrity and transparency for every community member: each investor is verified and signs a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) before gaining access to any startup information.

Join the exclusive ELYQO club and gain access to dozens of the most promising innovative startups.
